At the report page, you see selected users to report. If you need to change your selection, then back out of the page into the selection screen.

Below the selected users, you can customize the body of the report, or attach a document to send to each student's academic mentors. You also have the ability to send the report anonymously if you so desire.

Note: Both the body of the report and an attached media are optional. Apart from what you say here, the mentors are implicitly sent a progress report of the student(s) in question.

Once you are happy with the report, you can send the report by clicking the button. The system will then sned your report to every mentor for the selected students.
