Revoking team teaching invitations

Whether it is due to an invited instructor taking too long to accept or reject an invitation or a mistake in sending a team teaching invitation, it is easy to revoke an invitation. You will be working with the "Revoke team teaching invitations" section of the page. If there are no pending invitations or previously accepted invitations, then it will look like the picture below:

If you have any pending or previously accepted invitations, then it will look like the picture below:

Select the invitations you desire to remove by clicking their checkboxes and clicking the "Remove" button below. Once the page reloads, a message should appear stating emails have been sent to the instructors of the sections your revoked that looks like the following:

Sections selected revoked will be processed within an hour of the invitation being revoked. All invited students and instructors will be unenrolled from the instructor's Moodle course.
