Introduction

Team teaching courses allows you to enroll students and instructors from other courses into a section you are teaching. This benefits those who want to team up with instructors teaching the same course or a completely different course especially interdisciplinary classes. Unlike cross listing courses, team teaching only enrolls students and instructors from multiple Moodle courses into a single course rather than consolidating them in a single Moodle course.

As team teaching should not be taken lightly, instructors extend team teaching invitations to others to team teach their sections with another. These invitations can be either accepted or rejected as the receiving instructor desires. Though team teaming your courses with other instructors has many benefits, it is inevitable that some individuals will want to undo their team teachings. Don't worry as it is possible to remove the team teachings between courses and have students and instructors unenrolled properly.

Terminology

team teaching invitation
Invitation extended from one instructor to another inviting the receiving instructor and his/her students to participate in the requesting instructor's Moodle course.

Restrictions

Only primary instructors of sections can cross list them

As team teaching a course is an important decision, the only primary instructors that can team teach them.

Return to the top

Only sections from the same semester can be cross listed

As Moodle will have course and student information from different semesters available to instructors, only sections from the same semester can be cross listed.

Return to the top

Invitated section must have a primary instructor

As primary instructors are the only ones who can accept or reject team teaching invitations, only sections with a primary instructor can be team taught.

Return to the top

Cannot team teach if you are the primary instructor of both sections

Team teaching cannot be between two sections where the primary instructor of both is the same person. If you want to combine your students from multiple sections that you teach, then try cross listing the sections.

Return to the top

Invitations must be accepted before students and instructors are enrolled

Team teaching invitations must be accepted by the instructor who received the invitation before the students and instructors will be enrolled into the requestor's section.
