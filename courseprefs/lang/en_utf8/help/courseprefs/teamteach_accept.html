Accepting or rejecting team teaching invitations

When an instructor sends a team teaching invitation, the invited instructor is notified about the invitation and must either accept or reject it. A listing of invitiations to accept or reject can be found in the "Accept/reject team teaching invitations" form. When there are no invitations awaiting approval, the form looks like the following picture:

After you have sent a team teaching invitation, the form will look like the following picture:

To accept or reject these team teaching invitations, you must select either the invitations to accept or reject and click the appropriate button. This is so you can accept or reject multiple invitations together. Once you have click either the "Accept" or "Reject" button, the selected invitations will be processed and a message should appear stating that emails have been sent to the instructors of the selected sections that you have accepted or rejected.
